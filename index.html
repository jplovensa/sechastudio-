01. The Designers

Daffa • The Architect of Form

Modern Elegance.

For those who love clean lines, clutter-free spaces, and a touch of everyday luxury. Daffa focuses on making your space feel expansive, calm, and effortlessly polished. By keeping the foundational elements pure, the room becomes a serene backdrop to your life.

Audina • The Soul of Space

Cozy & Functional.

For those who want their home to feel like a warm hug. Audina blends Japandi warmth with practical, user-first design. She creates layouts that flow naturally with your daily routine, using warm palettes and inviting textures that make a house truly feel like a home.

02. How We Work Together

01

Getting to Know You

We start with a conversation. We listen to your needs, understand how you live, and align our vision with your budget and space realities. No pressure, just planning.

02

The Vision

Your ideas turn into a visual plan. We create bespoke moodboards and clear 2D layouts so you can see exactly how the space will be utilized.

03

Bringing It to Life

No more guessing. We provide high-quality 3D renders that show you exactly what your finished room will look like before you spend a dime on furniture or construction.

04

The Blueprint

We handle the technical details. You receive detailed engineering drawings (DED) so that contractors know exactly how to build the design flawlessly.

05

Making It Happen

The final handover. You can take our plans to your own builder, or transition seamlessly into our bespoke furniture production to let us finish the job.
